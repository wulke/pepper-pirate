# MARKET — Market and Trading Specifications

Status: `draft`
@source: [PRD.md](../PRD.md), [season-cycle.md](../process-flows/season-cycle.md)

---

### MARKET-001
The game shall fix market prices for all tradeable items (seeds, raw peppers, processed products) for the duration of each season — prices shall not fluctuate in real time within a season.

> **Testable:** The price of any item must be identical at season start and season end; no event or elapsed time may change it mid-season.

### MARKET-002
WHEN a season ends and the next season begins, the game shall set new market prices — creating a planning delta between seasons.

### MARKET-003
WHEN a player views market data, the game shall provide forecast information about future season conditions to support multi-season planning.

### MARKET-004
The game shall price seeds and peppers with rare or unique genetics at a premium over commodity-grade equivalents.

### MARKET-005
The game shall support buying and selling seeds, whole peppers, and processed products as distinct tradeable categories.
